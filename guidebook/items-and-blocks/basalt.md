# Basalt
Basalt is a naturally occurring dark-colored stone block that can be found in the overworld and nether.

### How to obtain
Basalt can be found in the overworld and nether, from natural world generation.

| Type          | Natual World Generation |
|---------------|-------------------------|
| Basalt        | `Yes`                   |
| Basalt Bricks | `No`                    |
